## Screen

To make an **overlay full screen** use `.xt-overlay-container.p-0` and `.xt-card.min-h-screen` and use **tailwind classes** (e.g. [align-items](https://tailwindcss.com/docs/align-items), [justify-content](https://tailwindcss.com/docs/justify-content)) to position `.xt-card` content.

## Position

To make an **overlay position** use `.xt-overlay-container.p-0` and `.xt-card.min-h-screen` and use **tailwind classes** (e.g. [max-width](https://tailwindcss.com/docs/max-width), [margin](https://tailwindcss.com/docs/margin)) on `.xt-overlay-container`.
